Here's little facts about me

I grew up in Manila, Philippines and migrated to Japan since 2005. I attended Secondary School in Colegio de San Juan de Letran in Intramuros, Manila where I first had a try on HTML & CSS.

I used to build computers for everyone as a sideline job, from computer for multimedia consumption, multimedia creation to gaming computers. It all started when I was 16 years old and had to replace the graphics card on my computer and it took me 2 hours, because I was afraid to break it. Since then I figured that it was only my fear that was holding me back to achieving my goal.

Interests: Computers, Guitars, Cars, Music, Watches & Food.

Trait: Motivated, Logical, Tinkerer.
